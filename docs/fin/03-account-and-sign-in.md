# Account and sign-in

## Sign-in options

Ration supports:

- **Google** sign-in (OAuth).
- **Magic link** email: you receive a link to sign in without a password.

Exact buttons and labels appear on the **landing and auth** screens in the app.

## After sign-in

- If you have more than one organization, you may be asked to **select a group** before opening the hub.
- New users may see an **onboarding tour** that highlights Cargo, Galley, Supply, and Manifest.

## Profile

From **Hub → Settings** you can update your **display name** and **avatar** (image types and size limits are shown in the UI). If you have not set a name, the app may show your email for clarity in shared groups.

## Email verification

Magic link flows use a verification path; follow the link in your email before it expires. If you do not receive mail, check spam and that you entered the correct address.

## Security basics

Sessions use secure cookies over HTTPS. Do not share your session with others; use **group invitations** for shared access instead.

If sign-in behavior differs from this article, follow the **in-app messages** or contact support.
