# Manifest (meal plan)

## Calendar and slots

The **Manifest** shows your week (navigate weeks with the in-app controls). Each day has **breakfast**, **lunch**, **dinner**, and **snack** slots. Add Galley meals to slots to build a plan.

## Servings override

Entries can carry a **servings override** for that occurrence so shopping and cook math reflect a different portion size than the recipe default.

## Readiness hints

Meal cards can show whether **ingredients look available** in Cargo (green vs needs-attention states). This is advisory—always verify quantities yourself before cooking.

## AI plan week

**Plan week** asks AI to draft a schedule from your Galley and preferences. It costs **3 credits**, runs **asynchronously**, and returns a **preview** you **confirm** before entries are bulk-added. Only meals in **your** Galley are eligible.

## Consume (cooked)

**Consume** marks selected plan entries as cooked and **deducts** matched ingredients from Cargo. Deduction uses careful semantic matching so only plausible pantry lines are reduced—when uncertain, the system prefers **not** to subtract the wrong item.

## Sharing

**Read-only sharing** of a meal plan link is available on **Crew Member** plans—see *Supply (shopping list)* and *Subscription tiers*.

If UI labels change, follow the **Manifest** screens in the app.
